Why Is Photography Important?
Photography, for me, is a way to see and preserve dynamic things statically, and come back to them as desired to reexamine them, alter them, or learn more from them. It is satisfying when it works, frustrating when it doesn't, and another excuse to engage. I believe that one idea, or one photograph, can rapidly change the whole world.
